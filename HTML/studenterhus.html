Studenterhus

Location

A lively student hub near Aarhus University, offering quizzes, bingo, live music, and a welcoming community for students and internationals.

The quiz

Studenterhus Aarhus hosts a variety of events, including lively bingo nights, live music performances featuring local and international talent, and an English quiz every other week. It’s a vibrant space for socializing, networking, and relaxing. For just 99 DKK, you can get a membership card, making beer only 25 DKK—perfect for enjoying the events!
